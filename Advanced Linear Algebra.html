M.Sc Mathematics Semester - 3

MSM4301 - Advanced Linear Algebra

Revision

  • Vector spaces.
  • Subspaces.
  • Bases and dimensions.
  • Dual spaces.
  • Linear transformations.

Unit - 1

  • The algebra of linear transformations.
  • Characteristic roots.
  • Matrices.

Unit - 2

  • Triangular canonical forms.
  • Nilpotent linear transformations.

Unit - 3

  • Trace and transpose.
  • A decomposition theorem.
  • Jordan canonical forms.

Unit - 4

  • Rational canonical forms.
  • Determinants.

Download Reference Books

  • The course is covered by the book Topics in Algebra 2nd edition - I N Herstein, John Wiley & Sons, Student Edition, New York. (2004).
  • Linear Algebra 2nd edition - Kenneth Hoffman & Ray Kunze, Prentice Hall of India,(1971).
  • A First course in linear algebra - P.B. Bhattacharya, Phani Bhushan Bhattacharya, S. K Jain, S. R. Nagpaul , New Age International Ltd Publishers, New Delhi. (2008).
  • Advanced linear algebra 3rd edition - Steven Roman, Springer. (2008).
