M.Sc Mathematics Semester - 3

MSM1301 - Functional Analysis - I

Unit - 1

  • Normed linear space: definition and examples.
  • continuous linear transformations.
  • Spaces BL(X,Y), BL(X) and BL(X,X).
  • lp & Lp (for 0 <= p <= N ) Banach spaces.

Unit - 2

  • Hahn-Banach theorem and its applications.
  • Open mapping theorem.
  • Dual normed spaces.
  • Natural imbedding of normed space into double dual space of normed spaces.

Unit - 3

  • Closed graph theorem.
  • Uniform boundedness principle.
  • Conjugate of an operator.
  • Bounded inverse mapping theorem.

Unit - 4

  • Hilbert space: definition and examples.
  • Orthogonal complement.
  • Orthonormal set.
