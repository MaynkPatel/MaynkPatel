M.Sc Mathematics Semester - 4

MSM1402 - Field Theory

Unit - 1

  • Extensions of field.
  • Finite extension.
  • Algebraic field extension.
  • Simple field extensions.
  • Transcendental numbers.

Unit - 2

  • Roots of polynomials.
  • The splitting field of a polynomial over a field.
  • Construction with straightedge and compass.

Unit - 3

  • The fixed field of a group of automophisms.
  • The theorem on symmetric polynomials.
  • Normal field extension.
  • The Galois group of a polynomial.

Unit - 4

  • The fundamental theorem of Galois theory.
  • Solvability by radicals.
  • Galois group over the rationals.
  • Finite fields.
