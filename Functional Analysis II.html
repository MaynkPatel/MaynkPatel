M.Sc Mathematics Semester - 4

MSM1401 - Functional Analysis - II

Unit - 1

  • Dual and transpose of a Hilbert spaces.
  • Adjoint of an operator.
  • Self-adjoint, normal.
  • Unitary operators.
  • Projections.

Unit - 2

  • Finite dimensional spectral theorem.
  • Weak convergence.

Unit - 3

  • Banach algebra: definition and examples.
  • Regular and singular elements.
  • Topological divisiors of zero.
  • Spectral of an element and spectral radius.
  • Radical and simplicity.

Unit - 4

  • Gelfand mapping.
  • Applications of the formula of the spectral radius.
  • involutions in Banach algebra.
  • Ideals in C(X), Banach-Stone theorem.
  • Commutative C* - Algebras.

Download Reference Books

  • The Course is Covered By Introduction to Topology and Modern Analysis - G. F. Simmons, McGraw Hill,1963
  • Functional Analysis 2nd Edition - B. V. Limaye, New Age International Limited, 2nd edition.
  • Banach Algebra - R Larson, Marcell Dekker,1973.
  • Automatic Continuity - H G Dales, Cambridge, 2000.
  • Lectures in Functional Analysis and Operator theory - S. K. Berberain, Springer Verlag.
  • First course in Functional Analysis - Goffman and George Padre, Prentice Hall of India.
  • Principles of Functional Analysis - Martin Schechter, Academic Press, New York.
