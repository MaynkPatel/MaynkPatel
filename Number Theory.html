M.Sc Mathematics Semester - 3

MSM1303 - Number Theory

Unit - 1

  • Divisibility.
  • G.C.D. , L.C.M.
  • Primes.
  • The fundamental theorem of arithmetic.
  • The Euclidean algorithm.
  • The greatest integer function.
  • The Mobius function (muyu).
  • The Euler function (fay).
  • The divisor functions σk for k >= 0 integer, properties of these functions.
  • Multiplicative functions.
  • Mobius inversion formula.

Unit - 2

  • Congruence.
  • Complete residue systems.
  • Linear Congruence.
  • Reduced residue systems.
  • Euler – Fermat theorem.
  • The Chinese remainder theorem.
  • The exponents of a number mod m.
  • Primitive roots.

Unit - 3

  • Quadratic Residues.
  • Gauss’ Lemma.
  • The quadratic reciprocity law.
  • The Jacobi Symbol.

Unit - 4

  • Diophantine Equations ax + by = c and its positive solutions.
  • the equation X2 + Y2 = Z2, the equation X4 + Y4 = Z2 and the equation X4 + Y4 = Z4.
  • Sum of squares.
  • The Fermat’s Last theorem.

Download Reference Books

  • An introduction to the theory of Numbers 3rd edition - I. Niven and H. Zukerman, Wiley Publication.
  • Introduction to Analytic Number Theorem - T. M. Apostol, Springer studt edition, 1995.
  • A concise Introduction to the theory of Numbers - Baker Alan, Cambridge University Press, 1984.
  • A course in number theory - Rose H. E., Oxford University Press, 1988.
  • Introduction to the theory of Numbers - Shapiro & Harold, John Wiley and Sons, 1983.
  • An Introduction to the theory of Numbers 5th edition - Hardy, G. H. and E. M. Wright, Oxford University Press, 1975.
  • Introduction to Number Theory 2nd edition - T. Nagell, chelsea, 1984.
